Privacy Policy (EN)

Controller. [Your company/personal name], email: support@example.com.

Purpose & Scope. Sound Meter uses the microphone solely to calculate sound level (dB, SPL) on-device and to display readings, a live chart, and history. Raw audio is neither stored nor transmitted off the device.

Data Categories. Sensor/Microphone data: processed locally and temporarily during measurement. No accounts, no sign-in, no personal data collection.

Sharing. We do not share data with third parties.

Retention. No raw audio is stored. Optional measurement history is stored locally and can be cleared in the app or by uninstalling.

Permissions. Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) is required to compute sound level. Other permissions, if used (notifications on Android 13+, vibration, wake lock), are only for proper UI behavior.

User Rights. You can clear local history, revoke microphone access in system settings, or uninstall the app. For privacy inquiries, contact the email above.

Children. General-audience app; not directed to children under 13.

Security. We do not transmit data to our servers. Keep the app and OS up to date.

Changes. We will update this page and the date above when changes occur.
